THE THINKING

When working with clients I will plan a project out to the mantra: Design, Develop, Deploy. I see these to be the three simple stages integral to a successful project, and simple is always better.

The design stage is where the bulk of the work lies, and where we assess the projects needs, what works, and what doesn't in order to get a projects' requirements down pat.

Once you're happy with the design we move to development. I firmly believe in keeping a constant feedback stream open between myself and my client - keeping you updated throughout development and also to ask you any questions or if we need any information we may have missed in the design phase.

The final phase is deployment, getting your new, awesome site, live. And in case of any hiccups, I keep the codebase and database backed up in source control and locally for extra piece of mind.

If a project isn't what you are needing I am also available for any bug fixing you might need, any refactoring or improvements to an existing codebase or project. I am also available for consultancy, training and code reviews.
